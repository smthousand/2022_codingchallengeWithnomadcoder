Best Curry in Seoul and Great Value! If you are looking for a change from traditional Korean food this is the answer. Upstairs above a Paris Patisserie it is simple, clean and comfortable with polite, relaxed service from one of the owners. We had the Everest Curry World set menu which was delicious. I am not usually a big fan of Naan but their Naan was exceptional. A fantastic "home cooked" meal at a very reasonable price. Make sure you try this restaurant.

Amazing food, prices, and service. I visited Everest Curry World for lunch on a Saturday and was extremely satisfied with my experience. I am a vegetarian and was happy to find a whole page of vegetarian dishes on their menu. Furthermore, their prices are about as good as you can get for a meal in Seoul. Entrees range from around 7,000-12,000 won. The curry was some of the best I've ever had, and the portion sizes were filling. Also the owners are a sweet Nepalese couple who talked with us for a few minutes. I can't wait to go again!
